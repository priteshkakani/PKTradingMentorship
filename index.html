Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the mentorship program

Do I need prior trading experience?

+

No prior experience is required. The program is designed to take you from beginner to advanced trader. We start with fundamentals and gradually build up to advanced strategies.

Will I trade from Day 1?

+

We follow a structured learning approach. You'll start with paper trading and simulations to build confidence before moving to live trading. This ensures you're well-prepared and understand risk management first.

What if I miss a live class?

+

All live classes are recorded and made available for lifetime access. You can watch them at your convenience and never miss any content. Additionally, you can ask questions about any recorded session via WhatsApp or phone.

Is support available daily?

+

Yes, you'll have access to WhatsApp and phone support throughout the program. While response times may vary, we ensure all queries are addressed promptly. During market hours, support is typically more immediate.

How do I enroll?

+

You can enroll by making the payment via UPI to priteshkakani@icici. After payment, share the transaction screenshot via WhatsApp or email (priteshkakani@outlook.com) to confirm your enrollment. You can also book a call to discuss the program before enrolling.

What are the minimum capital requirements?

+

There's no minimum capital requirement to join the program. However, for live trading, we recommend starting with capital you can afford to risk. We'll guide you on position sizing and risk management based on your capital. The focus is on learning and building skills first.
